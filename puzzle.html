Puzzles

  • Amazing Powers

    There are only three numbers that can be written as the sum of fourth powers of their digits:

    In this problem you have to find the sum of all the numbers that can be written as the sum of fifth powers of their digits.

    for example :

    Here you can see each number is written as sum of fourth power of sime digit

    1634 = 14 + 64 + 34 + 44
    8208 = 84 + 24 + 04 + 84
    9474 = 94 + 44 + 74 + 44

    The sum of these numbers is 1634 + 8208 + 9474 = 19316.
